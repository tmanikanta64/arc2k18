ABOUT

We are glad to inform you about the upcoming national level technical symposium to be held at ANITS ENGINEERING COLLEGE being conducted by the Department of Electrical & Electronics Engineering on 6th and 7th March.

The vibrant and energetic title ‘ARC’ signifies the sparkling efforts of the participants. It provides the ideal platform for all the students to analyze themselves technically and present their skills. Its tagline “Joining elites ” represents a special group of multi talented people and we felicitously invite you all to link up with the elite group and get the best out of it.
